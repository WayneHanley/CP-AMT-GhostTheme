What can AMT do to help you recover?

If you’re having muscular issues, repetitive strain injury (RSI), or struggling with pain, e.g. lower back, or neck and shoulder pain. Then book an appointment for a consultation and treatment. At AMT we use techniques used physiotherapists, osteopaths, sports massage therapists, chiropractors and Thai masseurs to deliver you the best possible treatment.

Do any of these sound familiar? Sciatica, lower back pain, stiff neck pain, tight muscles, soreness, muscle aches and spasms, restrictions in movement/mobility, tension headaches. We could help you.
